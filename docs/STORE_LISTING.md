# Store Listing — ZenJournal: AI Daily Journal

## Google Play

### Title (30자)
ZenJournal: AI Daily Journal

### Short Description (80자)
Smart AI journal with mood tracking, encrypted backup & personalized reflections

### Long Description
ZenJournal is an AI-powered journal that truly understands your writing.

Every day, it analyzes the emotions in your journal entries and provides personalized AI reflections. Unlike other journal apps, ZenJournal's AI actually works — it uses your writing history as context to generate specific, meaningful feedback.

**Core Features:**
- AI Reflection: Personalized insights based on your journal content
- Mood Tracking: 5-level emotion tracking with weekly/monthly trend visualization
- Encrypted Backup: Local AES-256 encryption keeps your data safe
- Daily Prompts: Fresh writing topics every day from 365+ curated prompts
- Streak System: Stay motivated with streak tracking, milestones, and badges

**Privacy First:**
Your journal entries are encrypted and stored on-device. Backup data supports end-to-end encryption. We never store your raw text on our servers.

**Free Features:**
- 1 journal entry per day (up to 500 characters)
- Basic mood tracking (5 levels)
- 1 daily writing prompt
- AI reflection (2x per week)
- Manual local backup
- Streak counter

**Pro Features:**
- Unlimited entries & character limit removed
- Unlimited AI reflections + deep analysis + weekly reports
- Custom emotions & detailed tags with correlation analysis
- Full history search + AI semantic search
- Automatic cloud backup with E2E encryption
- Export to PDF, TXT, JSON, CSV
- 10 premium themes + dark mode
- AI-personalized prompts
- Ad-free experience

Start your mindful journaling journey today.

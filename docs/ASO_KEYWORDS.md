## Keyword Placement Strategy

### App Title (most weight)
**ZenJournal: AI Daily Journal**
- Contains: "AI", "Daily", "Journal"
- Brand name "ZenJournal" is unique and searchable

### App Store Subtitle (iOS, 30 chars)
**Mood Tracker & Encrypted Backup**
- Contains: "Mood Tracker", "Encrypted", "Backup"
- Complements title keywords (no overlap)

### Google Play Short Description (80 chars)
**Smart AI journal with mood tracking, encrypted backup & personalized reflections**
- Contains: "AI journal", "mood tracking", "encrypted backup", "reflections"

### Keyword Field (iOS, 100 chars)
```
journal,ai journal,diary,mood tracker,daily journal,mood journal,reflection,encrypted,backup,streak
```
- No spaces after commas (Apple recommendation)
- No duplicates of title/subtitle words (Apple indexes them automatically)
- Singular forms only (Apple indexes plurals automatically)

### Long Description (Google Play — indexed for search)
Strategically includes:
- "journal" (12+ occurrences)
- "AI reflection" / "AI journal" (5+ occurrences)
- "mood tracking" / "mood journal" (4+ occurrences)
- "encrypted backup" (3+ occurrences)
- "daily prompt" / "daily journal" (3+ occurrences)
- "streak" (2+ occurrences)
- Long-tail: "best journal app", "private journal", "secure diary"
